# Dashboard Preview

## Executive Dashboard

![Executive Dashboard](powerbi/executive%20dashboard.png)

### Executive Insights

- Generated **$13.49M** in total revenue from **98K orders**.
- Served approximately **95K unique customers**.
- Maintained an average customer rating of **4.04 / 5**, indicating strong customer satisfaction.
- Average Order Value (AOV) is **137.42**.
- **Credit Card** is the most preferred payment method, contributing nearly **79%** of total revenue.
- **São Paulo (SP)** is the highest revenue-generating state with over **5.2M** in sales.
- Product categories such as **Health & Beauty**, **Watches & Gifts**, and **Bed & Bath** contribute the highest revenue.
- Customer review scores are heavily concentrated around **5 stars**, reflecting positive customer experiences.

---

## Customer Analytics Dashboard

![Customer Analytics](powerbi/customer%20analytics.png)

### Customer Insights

- Approximately **95K active customers** were analyzed.
- Only **3.03%** of customers made repeat purchases, indicating a very low customer retention rate.
- The average customer places **1.03 orders**, showing that most customers purchase only once.
- The average customer spend is **142.07**.
- **One-time customers account for 96.96% (92K)** of the customer base.
- One-time customers generate the highest revenue (**12.7M**) due to their large population.
- Repeat customers contribute only **0.7M** in revenue.
- Customer retention should be the primary business focus to improve Customer Lifetime Value (CLV).

The dashboard answers several important business questions:

- Which states generate the highest revenue?

- What is the average customer rating?
- How much revenue does each customer segment generate?
- What percentage of customers return to make another purchase?
- Which customer segments require retention strategies?
- How are sales changing over time?
